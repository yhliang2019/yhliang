About me

My name is LIANG Yuhong. I received the bachelor's degree in computer science from South China University of Technology in 2018. I am currently a Ph.D candidate in the department of computer science and engineering from Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Yang Ming-Chang. My research mainly focuses on emerging non-volatile memory, storage systems, and the next-generation storage architecture designs.
